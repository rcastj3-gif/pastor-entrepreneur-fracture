Questions Before You Decide

Do I have to be a pastor for this to work?

No. The Diagnostic works for anyone with authority in one context (church, speaking, credentials) who wants to build sales in another context. The fracture is real for anyone straddling these two worlds.

What if my problem is just that I need better ads or a better funnel?

You might be right. But most pastor-entrepreneurs think it's ads or funnel when it's actually positioning. The diagnostic will tell you for sure. If it's a positioning problem, all the ads in the world won't fix it.

How long does it take to see results?

Most people see immediate clarity within 1-2 weeks. Real revenue impact usually shows up within 30-60 days once you start implementing. Some people see closes within 2-3 weeks.

Will the diagnostic tell me what offer to build?

Not directly. But it will tell you what your market actually needs to hear before they'll buy anything. So you'll know which direction to go and exactly how to position it.
