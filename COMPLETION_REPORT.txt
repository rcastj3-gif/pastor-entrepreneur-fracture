================================================================================
KEY DESIGN ELEMENTS
================================================================================

1. GOLD (#d4af37) — The Authority Color
   Why: Historically associated with divine authority, clergy, wisdom
   Used: Primary CTAs, card borders, accents, hover states, pricing
   Impact: Users perceive authority → trust → higher conversion

2. DEEP CHARCOAL (#1a1a1a) — The Ground Truth Color
   Why: Serious, grounded, excellent contrast with gold
   Used: Hero background, primary text, dark cards
   Impact: Depth, seriousness, professional feel

3. GENEROUS WHITESPACE
   Why: Premium brands use whitespace (not cramped)
   Used: Between sections (6rem), inside cards (2-3rem)
   Impact: Feels premium, intentional, not rushed

4. SERIF TYPOGRAPHY (Playfair Display)
   Why: Classical, authoritative, pastoral connection
   Used: All headings, logo, numbers, prices
   Impact: Immediate signal of authority and depth

5. MICRO-INTERACTIONS
   Button Hover: Darkens + lifts (-2px) + shadow glows
   Card Hover: Lifts (-4px) + shadow deepens
   Input Focus: Border gold + subtle glow
   Impact: Design feels "alive", responsive, premium
